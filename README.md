# 🌸 Should I Buy It?

A cute, slightly existential calculator that converts impulse purchases into hours of your life.

## ✨ Features

💸 Convert item price into hours and minutes of work

💼 Supports both hourly pay and yearly salary

☕ Fun comparisons to make the cost more tangible:

- coffees

- Spotify subscriptions

- therapy sessions

- personal training sessions

- percentage of a weekend trip fund

🎀 Cute pastel UI with bows, flowers, hearts, and sparkles

🖌 Hand-drawn decorative elements

🌸 Lightweight – no frameworks, no backend

## 🧮 How It Works

1. Enter the price of the item

2. Choose your pay type

- Hourly pay

- Yearly salary

3. Enter your pay amount

4. Click Calculate

The app converts yearly salary to an hourly rate using the assumption:

- 2080 working hours per year

Then it calculates:

- hours = price / hourly_rate
- minutes = hours * 60

The price is also translated into relatable equivalents like coffee or Spotify.

## ⚠️ Note

This is an approximate calculation.

The app intentionally does not account for taxes, bonuses, or deductions because the goal is to provide a simple mental model, not a full financial analysis.
